Busiate con pesto alla trapanese

Home

Description

The Busiate con pesto alla trapanese is a traditional dish from Trapani's Provence.

The typical curly pasta is seasond with a pesto made out of basil, almomnds, garlic, fresh cherry tomatoes and of course lots of olive oil!

Ingredients for 4 servings:

  • 400g of fresh busiate
  • 50g of fresh basil
  • 100g of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 40g of toasted almonds
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 200g of diced cherry tomatoes
  • 5g salt

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with salted water and bring it to boil.
  2. Meanwhile prepare the pesto, in a mixing bowl add the olive oil, salt, basil, almonds, garlic and cheese.
  3. Blend until combined than transfer in a bowl.
  4. Add the diced cherry tomatoes to the pesto.
  5. Throw the pasta in the water and when al dente, drain it in the bowl with the pesto and mix.
